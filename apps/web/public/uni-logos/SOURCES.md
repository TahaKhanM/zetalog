# University logo provenance

Every asset in this directory is a square avatar-style mark published by the
university itself on its own web properties (brand/press pages or self-served
icon assets: apple-touch-icons, web-app-manifest icons, high-resolution
favicons). Nothing here comes from LinkedIn, Wikipedia, or any other
third-party platform or aggregator. Universities whose own properties publish
no usable square mark at >= 96 px keep their monogram chip (see
`apps/web/lib/uni-brand.ts` — removing a `CURATED_LOGOS` entry is the
per-university kill-switch).
